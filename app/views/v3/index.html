T Levels - The next level qualification

A headstart to your career

Coming in September 2020, T Levels are a new qualification that will help you build the future you want.

I'm a student

Your future workforce

T Levels include a 45-day industry placement that can help you find and develop new talent.

I'm an employer

80% CLASSROOM,
20% WORK

T Level students spend 80% of the course in the classroom, learning the skills that employers need. The other 20% is a real-world industry placement, where students put these skills into action.

EQUIVALENT TO 3 A LEVELS

T Levels are an alternative to A levels that focus on vocational skills. Equivalent to 3 A levels, a T Level can help students into skilled employment, university or a higher-level apprenticeship.

A 45 day industry placement

Each T Level includes a real-world placement that lasts at least 45 days. Students get hands-on experience in the workplace; employers get early sight of the new talent in their sector.
