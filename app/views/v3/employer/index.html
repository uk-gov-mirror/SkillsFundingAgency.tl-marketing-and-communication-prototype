MEET THE WORKFORCE
OF THE FUTURE

T Levels are new qualifications that have been designed with employers. One T level is equivalent to 3 A Levels and will give young people the skills, knowledge and attitude to thrive in the workplace.

At the heart of each course, a 45-day industry placement will give you early access to the brightest talent entering your market.

Find out more about T Levels

INDUSTRY PLACEMENTS ARE HAPPENING NOW

To prepare for T Levels, employers across England are trialling industry placements to spot and nurture young talent.

You can join this vocational revolution right now, with students ready to start placements in areas like:

  • construction
  • digital
  • education and childcare
  • health and science
