MEET THE WORKFORCE OF THE FUTURE

T Levels are new qualifications that have been designed with employers. One T level is the equivalent to 3 A Levels and will give young people the attitude and skills to thrive in the workplace.

At the heart of each course, a 45-day industry placement will give you early access to the brightest talent entering your market.

Find out more about T Levels

INDUSTRY PLACEMENTS ARE HAPPENING NOW

To prepare for T Levels, employers across England are trialling industry placements to spot and nurture young talent.

You can join this vocational revolution right now, with students ready to start placements in areas like:

  • construction
  • digital
  • education and childcare
  • health and science
Check what skills are available

BUSINESS BENEFITS

T Levels are part of a revolution in technical education. They address the skills gap by developing competent and work-ready young people.

The specific benefits of industry placements include:

  • spot and nurture young talent
  • develop an effective recruitment pipeline
  • access new ideas and cutting-edge skills

View all the business benefits of industry placements

THE BOTTOM LINE

Industry placements are a cost-effective way to bring new talent into your organisation. You'll be supported throughout the process by the college or school, which helps to reduce the time and effort required to manage a placement student.
