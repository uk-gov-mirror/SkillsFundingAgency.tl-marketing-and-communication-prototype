Equivalent to 3 A Levels

T Levels are an alternative to A levels that focus on vocational skills. Equivalent to 3 A levels, a T Level will help you into skilled employment, university or a higher-level apprenticeship.

Designed by employers

80% of each course is spent in the classroom, where students learn the skills that employers need. The remaining 20% is a real-world industry placement, where students put these skills into action.

A 45 day industry placement

Each T Level includes a placement that lasts at least 45 days. Students get hands-on experience in the workplace; employers get early sight of the new talent in their sector.
