Meet the workforce of the future

T Levels are new qualifications designed with employers to give young people the knowledge and skills your business needs.

They combine classroom learning with real-world industry placements, covering areas from health and construction to media and science.

Find out more about T Levels

A skilled industry placement

Industry placements are happening now in readiness for the launch of T Levels. Employers like you are already finding them a great way to spot and nurture young talent.

They last a minimum of 315 hours (around 45 days) and are arranged in partnership with a local college or school.
