Tomorrow's talent today

In September 2020, the introduction of T Levels into schools and colleges will create a new route for employers to attract and develop new talent. It's the biggest change to vocational training in a generation, and the business benefits are huge. But are they a good fit for you?

Are T Levels right for my organisation?

Business Benefits

Every T Level includes an industry placement lasting at least 315 hours (45 days). These will help you:

  • find cost-effective solutions for entry-level jobs
  • develop new talent with fresh, imaginative ideas
  • build partnerships with local schools and colleges
  • improve the diversity of your workforce
