About T Levels

In September 2020, we launch T Levels: a new qualification for students who've finished GCSEs. They'll be the biggest reform to vocational training in a generation, giving young people the attitude, skills and experience to thrive in the workplace.

DESIGNED BY EMPLOYERS FOR EMPLOYERS

In a competitive global marketplace, British employers want young recruits that can adapt quickly to the challenges of work. We've worked with employers to make sure T Levels are aligned with the skills that your industries need.

A SIMPLER APPROACH TO VOCATIONAL TRAINING

We know employers find the current range of vocational training options confusing, and that the courses aren't always fit for purpose. T Levels will change this by providing a clear and high quality, technical alternative to A levels. They're part of our strategy to simplify vocational training in the UK and create a system that you can trust and understand.

INTRODUCING INDUSTRY PLACEMENTS

Every T Level student completes an industry placement that lasts a minimum of 315 hours (or 45 days). Industry placements give you a unique opportunity to help develop new talent and get young people work ready. They also bring many business benefits.

ARE THEY RIGHT FOR YOU?

In preparation for T Levels, employers are already offering industry placements across a range of skills - from creative, design and digital to construction, engineering and manufacturing. If you think industry placements could help your organisation find and develop new talent, check what skills are available.
