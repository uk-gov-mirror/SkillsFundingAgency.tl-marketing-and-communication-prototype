I'm an employer

Equivalent to 3 A Levels

After GCSE's, students can choose to study a T Level. Lasting 2 years, it carries the same weight as 3 A Levels, and opens up opportunities to continue into skilled employment, university or a higher-level apprenticeship.

80% Classroom; 20% Workplace

Employers have helped create the T Level course content so that students learn the skills that industry needs. A quarter of every course is spent in the workplace, putting these skills into action.

A 45 day industry placement

Each T Level includes a placement lasting at least 315 hours (45 days). Students get hands-on experience of working in their chosen area. Employers get an early sight of the new talent coming into their sector.
