Industry placements

An industry placement lasting at least 315 hours makes up 20% of every T Level. Students get hands-on experience of working in their chosen area. Employers get early sight of the new talent coming into their sector. Together they can start to build a stronger workforce for the future.

Good for business

For employers, industry placements are:

  • - a source of new talent with fresh, imaginative ideas
  • - a way to improve staff's mentoring and management skills
  • - a cost-effective option for entry-level jobs
  • - a chance to build partnerships with local schools and colleges

Find out more about industry placements (on GOV.UK)

Great for your career

For students, industry placements will:

  • - improve the knowledge and skills you need to get a job
  • - help you understand the world of work
  • - expand your professional network
  • - give you a competitive advantage over your peers in the job market
