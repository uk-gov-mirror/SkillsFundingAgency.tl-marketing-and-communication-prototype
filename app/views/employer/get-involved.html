3 WAYS TO GET INVOLVED

The vision for T Levels is a complete re-engineering of vocational education so that modern Britain can compete globally. Its success relies on employers of all shapes and sizes engaging with vocational training on a scale that this country has never seen before.

1

START PREPARING FOR T LEVEL PLACEMENTS

In September 2020, more than 2000 students will start T Level courses in:

  • Design, Surveying and Planning (in the construction route)
  • Digital Production, Design and Development (in the digital route)
  • Education (in the education and childcare route)

In preparation, T Level schools and colleges are already talking to local employers about delivering industry placements.

If you want to be involved in the first wave of T Level placements, find T Level schools and colleges near you

OFFER INDUSTRY PLACEMENTS NOW

To give T Levels the greatest chance of success, the government is running a special scheme to give employers, schools and colleges practice in delivering industry placements.

Right now, students from more than 300 schools and colleges across England are looking to do industry placements in a wide range of skill areas.

If there are no relevant T Levels being rolled out in your area or you just want to start building your organisation's industry placement capability, we can introduce you to local schools and colleges.

3

SPREAD THE WORD

If you feel passionately about the potential benefits of T Levels for British employers, you can join the T Level ambassador network.

In addition to offering industry placements, ambassadors:

  • encourage other employers to offer industry placements
  • speak at stakeholder events about the benefits of industry placements
  • promote T Levels through social media channels, blogs and opinion pieces
  • gather feedback from local employers as well as schools and colleges on T Level policy

Find out how to join the T Level ambassador network.

STILL NEED MORE INFORMATION?

Visit GOV.UK to learn more about:

  • the introduction of T Levels
  • industry placements

The Strategic Development Network has produced a range of government-funded support materials to help employers prepare for T Level industry placements.
